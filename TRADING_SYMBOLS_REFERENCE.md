## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notes

### Alpaca API Support
- ‚úÖ **Supported**: US stocks, ETFs, and some options
- ‚úÖ **Market Hours**: 9:30 AM - 4:00 PM ET (regular trading hours)
- ‚ùå **Not Supported**: International stocks directly (use ETFs instead)
- ‚ùå **Not Supported**: OTC/Pink Sheet stocks
- ‚ùå **Not Supported**: Crypto (separate API)

### International Stocks
**Direct international stocks are NOT supported by Alpaca API.** However, you can trade international exposure through International ETFs (see section above for full list).

**Why ETFs instead of direct stocks?**
- Alpaca only supports US-listed securities
- International ETFs trade on US exchanges (NYSE, NASDAQ)
- ETFs provide diversification across multiple international companies
- Better liquidity and lower trading costs
- No currency conversion needed

**Example International Portfolio:**
```
SPY,EWJ,FXI,EWZ,EWU,EWG,EEM
```

### OTC/Pink Sheet Stocks
**OTC (Over-The-Counter) and Pink Sheet stocks are NOT supported by Alpaca API.**

**What are OTC/Pink Sheet stocks?**
- Stocks that trade outside major exchanges (NYSE, NASDAQ)
- Often have lower liquidity and higher volatility
- May have less regulatory oversight
- Examples: Penny stocks, micro-cap companies

**Why not supported?**
- Alpaca focuses on liquid, exchange-traded securities
- OTC stocks have limited market data availability
- Higher risk and compliance requirements
- Execution can be unreliable

**Alternatives:**
- Trade small-cap ETFs instead: **IWM** (Russell 2000), **VB** (Vanguard Small-Cap ETF)
- Trade micro-cap ETFs: **IWC** (iShares Micro-Cap ETF)
- Focus on NASDAQ/NYSE listed small-cap stocks that ARE supported

**Supported Small-Cap Alternatives:**
- **IWM** - iShares Russell 2000 ETF (Small cap)
- **VB** - Vanguard Small-Cap ETF
- **IWC** - iShares Micro-Cap ETF
- **IJR** - iShares Core S&P Small-Cap ETF
- **SCHA** - Schwab U.S. Small-Cap ETF

### Symbol Format
- Use **uppercase** symbols (e.g., `TSLA` not `tsla`)
- No spaces in the comma-separated list
- Maximum recommended: 10-20 symbols (to avoid rate limits)

## üöÄ Quick Setup

1. Go to GitHub ‚Üí Your Repository ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Secrets
2. Edit or create `TRADING_SYMBOLS`
3. Paste your comma-separated list (e.g., `SPY,TSLA,AAPL`)
4. Save
5. The next GitHub Actions run will trade all symbols!

## üí° Tips

- **Start Small**: Begin with 1-3 symbols to test
- **Monitor Performance**: Check which symbols perform best
- **Diversify**: Mix indexes, sectors, and individual stocks
- **Consider Liquidity**: Popular symbols (SPY, QQQ, AAPL) have better execution
- **Watch Rate Limits**: Too many symbols may hit API limits
